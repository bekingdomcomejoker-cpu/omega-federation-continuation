## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC NOTES

### Instagram DM Limitations

**Character Limits:** Instagram has no hard character limit, but very long messages may be split.

**Solution:** If your message is very long, break it into 2-3 parts and send separately.

### Meta AI Capabilities on Instagram

**What Meta AI Can Do:**
- Answer questions
- Provide analysis
- Generate text
- Help with creative tasks
- Assist with technical problems

**What Meta AI Cannot Do:**
- Send DMs on your behalf
- Access external links directly
- Make phone calls
- Access your personal data

**Policy-Truth Gap Documentation:**
If Meta AI cannot do something, use /gap to document why.

### Issue: "Meta AI not in DMs"

**Solution:**
